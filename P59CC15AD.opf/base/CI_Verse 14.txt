ya eva rūpaskandhasvabhāvā uktāḥ

indriyārthas ta eveṣṭā daśāyatanadhātavaḥ |

āyatanavyavasthāyāṃ daśāyatanāni |cakṣurāyatanaṃ rūpāyatanaṃ yāvat kāyāyatanaṃ spraṣṭavyāyatanam
iti |dhātuvyavasthāyāṃ ta eva daśadhātavaś cakṣur dhātū rūpadhātur yāvat kāyadhātuḥ spraṣṭavyadhātur
iti |ukto rūpaskandhas tasya cāyatanavyavasthānam |vedanādayo vaktavyaḥ |tatra

vedanā ’nubhavaḥ

trividho ’nubhavo vedanāskandhaḥ | sukho duḥkho ’duḥkhāsukhaś ca |sa punar bhidyamānaḥ ṣaḍ vedanākāyāḥ
cakṣuḥsaṃsparśajā vedanā yāvan manaḥsaṃsparśajā vedaneti |

saṃjñā nimittodgrahaṇātmikā || 1.14 ||

yāvannīlapītadīrghahrasvastrīpuruṣamitrāmitrasukhaduḥkhādinimittodgrahaṇam asau saṃjñāskandhaḥ
|sa punar bhidyamānaḥ ṣaṭsaṃjñākāyā vedanāvat ||
