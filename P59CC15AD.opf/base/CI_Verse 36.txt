aṣṭādaśānaṃ dhātūnāṃ kaś chīnatti kaś chidyate ko dahati ko dahyate kas tulayati kas tulyate
|

chinatti cchidyate caiva bāhyaṃ dhātuṃ catuṣṭayam |

rūpagandharasaspraṣṭavyākhyaṃ paraśudārvādisaṃjñakam | chedo nāma ka eṣa dharmaḥ |saṃbandhotpādinaḥ
saṃghātasrotaso vibhaktotpādanam |na kāyendriyādīni cchidyante |niravaśeṣāṅgacchede
tadadvaidhīkaraṇāt |na hīndriyāṇi dvidhā bhavanti |chinnasyāṅgasya nirindriyatvāt |na cāpi
cchindanti | maṇiprabhāvad andhatvāt |yathā cchinatti cchidyate caiva bāhyaṃ dhātucatuṣṭayam
|

dahyate tulayaty evaṃ

tad eva dahyate tad eva tulayati | nendriyāṇy acchatvān maṇiprabhāvat | na śabda uccheditvāt
|

vivādo dagdhṛtulyayoḥ || 1.36 ||

kecid āhuḥ tad eva dhātucatuṣṭayaṃ dāhakaṃ tulyaṃ cakecid āhus tejodhātur eva dagdhā gurutvam
eva ca tulyam iti |
